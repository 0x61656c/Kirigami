ABOUT THE PROJECT

I've been interested in Origami since I was a child. In 2018, I was out to dinner and folded a paper box. I cut out a slit reminiscent of a door and asked myself the following:

Why can't we scale this thing up, waterproof it, and use it for emergency relief housing in post natural-disaster scenarios?

In the interest of curiousity, I've been exploring that question since August of 2018. My research on the topic has led me to believe that this is actually a commercially viable idea, with possible applications as both a non and for-profit project.
